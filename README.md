# Coffee Shop Sales Analysis
📊 **Excel Dashboard Project**

## 📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates advanced Excel analytics by analyzing transactional sales data from a multi-location coffee shop chain. The analysis uncovers actionable insights about revenue trends, customer behavior patterns, and product performance across three store locations.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Identified revenue growth patterns and peak business periods
- Quantified product category performance and customer preferences
- Enabled data-driven decision-making through interactive dashboards

## 📊 Key Insights & Findings

### 💰 Revenue Performance (Jan–Jun)
- **Total Revenue:** $230,057.25
- **Highest Revenue Month:** June ($54,445.69)
- **Trend:** Consistent growth from March to June (+16.2% increase)
- **Implication:** Strong upward momentum entering peak season

### 📅 Transaction Volume by Day of Week
- **Highest:** Monday (7,136 transactions)
- **Lowest:** Sunday (6,679 transactions)
- **Key Insight:** Weekday demand outperforms weekends by ~6.8%
- **Implication:** Staffing and inventory should prioritize weekday operations

### ⏰ Peak Transaction Hours
- **Peak Hour:** 10 AM (6,297 transactions)
- **Peak Period:** 7 AM–10 AM (Morning rush)
- **Implication:** Morning coffee demand drives 40%+ of daily traffic
- **Recommendation:** Staff optimization needed during morning hours

### ☕ Product Category Performance
| Category | Transaction Volume | Market Share |
|----------|-------------------|--------------|
| Coffee | 18,204 | 50.8% |
| Tea | 13,912 | 38.8% |
| Bakery | 7,890 | 22.0% |

**Finding:** Coffee is the clear revenue driver, representing over half of all transactions.

### 🏆 Top Performing Products
1. **Barista Espresso** – $31,051.00 (Revenue Leader)
2. **Gourmet Brewed Coffee** – $23,201.20
3. **Additional high-performers** driving significant margin contribution

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. **Staffing:** Increase morning shift coverage (7 AM–10 AM) to handle peak demand
2. **Inventory:** Prioritize coffee stock; ensure adequate supply during peak hours
3. **Location Strategy:** Leverage high-performing locations as best-practice models
4. **Seasonal Planning:** Capitalize on summer momentum with targeted promotions
5. **Product Mix:** Focus marketing efforts on high-margin espresso-based drinks
