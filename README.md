# coffee_shop_analysis
📊 Coffee Shop Sales Analysis (Excel Dashboard Project)
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes transactional sales data from a coffee shop chain to uncover insights about:

Revenue trends over time

Transaction patterns by day and hour

Product category performance

Top-performing products

Location-based filtering (Astoria, Hell’s Kitchen, Lower Manhattan)

The dashboard was built entirely in Microsoft Excel using Pivot Tables, Slicers, and Data Visualization tools.

🛠 Tools & Skills Used

Microsoft Excel

Pivot Tables

Slicers

Data Cleaning

Aggregations (SUM, COUNT)

Dashboard Design

Business Insight Interpretation

📈 Key Business Questions Answered

How does revenue change month over month?

Which day of the week has the highest transactions?

What are peak transaction hours?

Which product categories drive the most sales?

What are the top 15 revenue-generating products?

How does performance vary by store location?

📊 Key Insights
💰 Revenue Trend (Jan–Jun)

Revenue increased consistently from March to June.

Highest revenue month: June ($54,445.69)

Total Revenue: $230,057.25

This indicates strong upward momentum entering summer months.

📅 Transactions by Day of Week

Highest transactions: Monday (7,136)

Lowest transactions: Sunday (6,679)

This suggests weekday demand is stronger than weekends.

⏰ Transactions by Hour

Peak hour: 10 AM (6,297 transactions)

Morning period (7 AM–10 AM) drives highest traffic.

This confirms the business is heavily dependent on morning coffee demand.

☕ Product Category Performance

Top categories by transaction volume:

Coffee – 18,204

Tea – 13,912

Bakery – 7,890

Coffee clearly dominates total sales volume.

🏆 Top Performing Products

Highest revenue product:

Gourmet Brewed Coffee – $23,201.20

Barista Espresso – $31,051.00

These products drive significant revenue contribution.
